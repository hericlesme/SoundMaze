SoundMaze

SoundMaze

is a trainable labyrinth where every movement is triggered by a sound.

How to Play?

To train the character, you need to click and hold the joystick button whose direction you want to train. While doing this, speak the sound you want the character to hear to perform that move.

Then, when you train all the moves, press the Train button to build the model.

When the model is trained, press the Listen button and enjoy.

Remember that you can stop and train your model again so that you can achieve a better result.
